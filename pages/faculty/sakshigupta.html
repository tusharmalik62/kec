Executive Summary (About Self)

Sakshi Gupta , Assistant Professor with the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at K.E.C Ghaziabad has an experience of 5.5 years and her specialized area is Image Processing. She has achieved B Tech (Honors) from University of Rajasthan, M Tech from Amity University, Noida and is pursuing PhD from B.R. Ambedkar Bihar University. She has been associated with organizations like IAENG, UACEE. She has strong record in both teaching and research. She has 6 months’ experience as a Trainee under Dr. Jagdish Lal Raheja working on government sponsored project at CEERI, Pilani. She has Hand-on experience on Arduino Kit, Xylinx, Matlab 2015. Her publications include an internationally referred Book and various papers at national and international platform. She is committed to offer knowledge to student and has expertise to help further their academic and professional goals. She has taught subjects like Digital Image Processing, Analog Electronics, Digital Electronics, Microprocessor, Microcontroller and always try to bring out best in students for their achievement.
