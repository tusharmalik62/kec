Executive Summary (About Self)

Deepak Kumar Gupta received the B.Sc degree in 1999 and M.Sc degree in 2001 from Dr R M L Avadh University Faizabad,(U.P.), India. He has received M.Tech degree in Electronics Design and Technology from Centre of Electronics Design and Technology of India, Gorakhpur, U.P. in 2003. He has been associated with Krishna Engineering College, Ghaziabad since last 8 years as an Asst. Prof. in Electronics and Communication Engineering Department. During this time span he has successfully completed the number of assignments at both the departmental level as well as college level. He worked as Coordinator for the departmental society “Electrophilla”, Head Proctor & Proctor, Project Coordinator, Attendance Coordinator etc. he has also worked as Assistant Centre Superintendent (ACS) in exam cell. He has organized two workshops “Embedded System Design” & “Digital Design using VHDL/VERILOG” in ECE department in collaboration with Industry. Mr Gupta guided one M Tech project and more than ten B tech level projects. He is doing PhD on topic “Low Complexity Non-Linear Acoustic Echo Cancellation System” in Adaptive Signal Processing Field. His keen research areas are speech, noise and echo signal processing. He has published seven research papers in good quality of Journals and International Conferences. His research interest includes signal and speech processing. He has called as a Guest Faculty in CSE Jhansi, MP and JIMTECH Institute Ghaziabad. He is known for his good knowledge in several fields like Microprocessors, Microcontrollers and DSP. He is a life member of ISTE, member of IACSIT.
