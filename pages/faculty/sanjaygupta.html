Executive Summary (About Self)

Prof. Sanjay Gupta joined the services of the College w.e.f. 22nd August’16 in Civil Engineering Department.
Prof. S. Gupta is an alumnus of BITS Pilani from where he did B.E. (Hons.) in Civil Engineering before completing Masters in Water Resources Engineering from NIT Kurukshetra.
He brings with him more than 15 years experience in infrastructure development works spanning World Bank and ADB Assisted water supply projects, and building works including IT Park for DMRC Ltd.
Prof. S Gupta has more than 7 years experience in teaching undergraduate students of leading universities and colleges in Delhi NCR and abroad.
His areas of interest encompass

  1. Construction Management
  2. Water Management
  3. Analysis and Design of Structures
  4. Concrete Technology
