Executive Summary (About Self)

He has more than 5 years of experience in teaching field. Currently serving as Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Krishna Engineering College, Ghaziabad since July 2015. He has authored more than 25 papers which have been published in many National and International journals and conferences including Elsevier and springer journal. His paper got more than 100 google scholar citation. He had also served as a Technical program committee (TPC) member and Editorial board member in international conferences and journals. He is serving as a Reviewer for IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics, Measurement Journal (Elsevier), International Journal of Engineering (IJE), Computers and Electrical engineering (Elsevier), International Journal of Scientific Engineering and Technology (IJSET), International Journal of Engineering Research and Technology (IJERT), Majlesi Journal of Electrical Engineering (MJEE). He is also an active member of various international Societies such as IEEE Professional Member, The Society of Digital Information and Wireless Communications (SDIWC) and International Association of Engineers (IAENG). His area of Expertise is Bio-signal processing, Control System, Image Processing, Neural Network, Pattern Classification and Artificial Intelligence.
