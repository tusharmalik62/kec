Executive Summary (About Self)

Saurabh Kumar Singh has an experience of 13 years in the academics and having expertise in Digital Signal Processing, Network analysis & Synthesis and in Electromagnetic field theory,VLSI & Signals and systems. He has hand on experience on tools of Primetime,TetraMax(Testing) , Mentor graphics simulation tool (version 5.4). He has been guided students under IEDC (MSME sponsered) projects (i.e.Oil snatcher).Other than teaching graduates he also conducted & taught GATE classes & Crash courses (Signals & systems and NAS).
