Executive Summary (About Self)

Ms. Umang Kant is Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Krishna Engineering College, Mohan Nagar, Ghaziabad since July, 2014. She obtained her M.Tech in Intelligent Systems & Robotics from School of ICT, Gautam Buddha University in July, 2013 and joined as Assistant Professorin Computer Science Department at University Institute of Engineering & Technology, Kurukshetra University. Prior to that she has worked as a Lecturer in Xavier Institute of Engineering, Mahim, Mumbai in Computers Department. She has about Five years of teaching and research experience. Her interests are in all fields of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Intelligent Systems (Expert Systems), Cloud Robotics, Pattern Recognition, Data Mining & Data Warehousing, Recommendation Systems. She has guided around 6 B.Tech Projectsand and 1 M.Tech thesis. She has two research papers published in journal and international conference. She has attended several workshops on various computer science as well as inter-diciplinarytopics.

She has held various administrative posts in Admissions Committee, Tech-Guru , Cultural Committee etc.
She is a member of ACM (Association for Computing Machinery).
