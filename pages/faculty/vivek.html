Executive Summary (About Self)

Prof. Sanjeev Kumar received Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical in 1998 and Master of Engineering in Mechanical from Delhi College of Engineering, Delhi and PhD in Mechanical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology (Indian School of Mines) Dhanbad. He has an excellent track record of Innovation and research with high quality teaching. He has also supervised more than 30 UG projects in the area of Mechanical Engineering. He has more than 55 publications in the national and International Journals/Conferences. Prof. Sanjeev Kumar is also supervising four Doctorate (Ph.Ds) in the field of Advance welding, Machining processes, Water jet machining and Characterization of wear behavior of piston rings coating by physical vapor deposition. Prof. Sanjeev Kumar has also authored a book on “Computer Applications”. He is also Reviewer of various International Journals. He has also participated and organized workshop /Seminar /FDP’s and conferences at prestigious Institutions. He has served as Officiating Director, Dean, and Head of the Department, controller of examinations, Incharge Training and placement cell.
