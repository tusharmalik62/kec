Executive Summary (About Self)

Mr. Vishwajeet Pattanaik is currently working as Assistant Professor in Computer Science Engineering Department at Krishna Engineering College, Ghaziabad (U.P.), India. He recently completed a 2 months Research Fellowship Program at Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, New Delhi. He has been an academician for over 3 years now and has Industry experience of around 1.5 years. He completed his Masters (M.Tech) with specialization in Knowledge Engineering (Artificial Intelligence) from SRM University, Chennai (T.N.), India in 2014. He obtained his graduation degree (B.Tech) in Computer Science Engineering form Uttar Pradesh Technical University, India in 2011. Vishwajeet has attended, participated and assisted in organizing several workshops and conferences organized both at SRM University and Krishna Engineering College. He recently served as a reviewer for IEEE TENCON 2016. He is currently also working as Organizing Secretary for International Conference on Advances in Computing and Data Sciences (ICACDS-2016). Vishwajeet is a member a of Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), Computer Society of India (CSI) and Association for Computing Machinery (ACM).
