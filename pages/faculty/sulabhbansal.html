Executive Summary (About Self)

Sulabh Bansal has total experience of around 16 years in the field of Computer Science and Engineering comprising of more than 7 years in Academics (Teaching and designing courses), over 6 Years in Industry (Designing, developing and managing the Software solutions and resources) and around 3 Years in Government funded Research Projects during Ph.D. He has developed teaching courses and taught subjects like Design and Analysis of Algorithms, Theory of Formal Automata, Operating Systems, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Networks, Database Management Systems, Programming Languages (Java, C), Compilers etc. He has development experience in diversified programming environments like Parallel programming using Open MP, MPI and CUDA Programming for GPU, Application Development using C, Java, .Net, MySQL, Sybase, etc. and Legacy Mainframe Environment (MVS-Assembler, COBOL, DB2, JCL, CICS, etc.). He has published several research papers in reputed International Journals. His research has 27 Citations till date and gained an H Index of 3. During his past experience, he has been associated with various reputed organization like Computer Sciences Corporation, Dayalbagh Educational Institute.
