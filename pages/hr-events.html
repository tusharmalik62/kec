Holi Celebration 2014

The dignitaries & KEC family were welcomed with Tilak by the welcome committee (Dr. Manishi Sharma, Ms. Madhu Bala Bhatt, Ms. Santosh Singhal & Ms. Priyanka Goyal). Ms. Swati Chaudhary & Dr. Lalit Gupta anchored the first session of the event creating a poetic environment and welcomed Hon`ble Director & KEC Family along with the lightning of lamp before Maa Saraswati marking the beginning of the celebration. To begin with the celebration, Lord Krishna was remembered through a beautiful song presented by Dr. Chetna Sharma. Various other musical events and Shero Shayari added spice to the event. To add some flavour to the event Dumb-Sheraz, Musical Chair and Tambola were organized.

Dr. Manoj Goel (CAO) joined the event & extended his wishes to the KEC family. “Lucky KEC Family member of the day" - Dr. A.B. Datye (Director,KEC) was presented the bumper prize by Dr. Manoj Goel.

The show was enjoyed by every member of the KEC family. Holi Wishes were given to all.
